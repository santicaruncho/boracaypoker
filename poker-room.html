Welcome to Boracay Live Poker Room

Poker Venue

The Boracay Poker Room has 16 poker tables to accommodate big tournament events and private poker tournaments. It offers games like No Limit Texas Holdem (NLH), Pot Limit Omaha (PLO), and other games upon request of the patron. Blind limit varies from Php50/100 and above.

Accommodations in Boracay

Boracay Poker has partnered with hotel establishments to ensure that international and local patrons feel comfort and relaxation during their trip to the island.Boracay Poker Player Club members get discounts in booking rooms in these hotels and in dining in partner restaurants.

Local Destinations

When visiting the Philippines, there are a lot of places to go to – whether you want to go to a decadent nightlife scene, a relaxing beach trip, an educational culture tour or just to go shopping. Remember, it’s always More Fun in the Philippines!
